Welcome!

I'm Anna Hong, MSW, RSW.

I'm a Registered Social Worker and Psychotherapist with ten years of experience specializing in care for mental health and recovery.

I'm dedicated to offering a therapeutic space that encourages honest exploration and self-discovery, helping people make sense of the patterns and experiences shaping their lives. By asking thoughtful, and sometimes difficult, questions, I strive to help people gain deeper insight into these challenges, equipping them with practical tools and strategies to foster meaningful and lasting change.

My approach

Empathy, understanding, and practical solutions

My goal is to support individuals in gaining insight, resolving conflicts, and improving overall mental health and well-being.
